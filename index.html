APS PANAGARH

APS panagarh

ARMY PUBLIC SCHOOL, PANAGARH started its humble journey in 1974 as a Nursery School with just 10 students and then was upgraded to Garrison School in 2001. It became Army School under the aegis of AWES. (Army Welfare Education Society) on 1st Apr 2006. In Jul 2011, the School was upgraded to Army Public School, Panagarh and was accorded the ‘Public School’ status.The School has classes from class I to XII with 44 teachers and 931 students. Class XI and XII have two streams - Science and Humanities.The global educational landscape is changing rapidly and has become more dynamic and vibrant than ever. We, at Army Public School, Panagarh offer an excellent environment for pursuit of academic excellence and personal development, so that an individual may achieve his or her full potential.The Institution not only imparts knowledge that encompasses the prescribed syllabi, but that of the world beyond the classroom which will prepare our students to become productive & responsible citizens of our nation.
